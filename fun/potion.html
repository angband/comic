Angbandish Fun (r.g.r.a highlights) - It's a hard life being a potion

I dropped 5 potions of cure light wounds in my house, but when I tried to pick them up, it said that I didn't have enough gold to buy them, from my own house?!

It's a hard life being a potion, you know. You get stuck in a cramped back-pac with battlestained weapons and smelly food. You get jostled around. I'm sure that when a potion finally gets a chance to relax and have some personal space, it isn't very keen on going back to the backpack. It's reasonable to pay it something for it's troubles.

The Angband Comic archives

This is an archive of an Angband inspired online comic strip written by Jon Cole and Devin Kent back in 2001 and 2002. The original website disappeared from the face of the web long time ago. I was unable to contact the authors, so I decided to make this gem available online again, even without their consent. I hope they will reappear one day.

I would like to thank Dominik who helped me with restoring this archive.
